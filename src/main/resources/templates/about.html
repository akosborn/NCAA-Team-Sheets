About

Quadrantology was created in order to provide easier viewing and access to the quadrant criteria introduced by this season as a new way to evaluate teams for the NCAA Tournament. The official team sheets can be found here in a PDF. As of now, the RPI calculated here reflects the most-recently-completed day's games.

If you have any suggestions or would like to help with the site in any way, contact me at admin@cbbquadrantology.com.
